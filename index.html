About me

Camila Gascon

Born in Sao Paulo - Brazil

Living in Boston, Ma - United States

My name is Camila Fuzihara Gascon, I’m a 28 years old architect, maker and currently learning about user experience design and how to code.
I consider myself a problem solver and I use observation and a human-centered approach to any challenge I face, I love technology that empowers people and bring them autonomy.  I was born in the São paulo - Brazil, a multicultural city that attracted my ancestors from Japan, Portugal and Spain. And I came to Boston in 2014, when my husband got a job as a data visualisation specialist, I have always dreamed about living in a safer city where I could bike everywhere, with more opportunities for growth. Here I created CAMI wooden things, a design studio that creates beautiful objects to make everyday life more enjoyable, and had the chance to be part of the Artisan’s Asylum maker space, which inspired me to learn about the challenges and resources of developing a great community culture.
